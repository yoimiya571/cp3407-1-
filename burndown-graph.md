# Iteration 1 Burn Down Graph

## Purpose

The burn down graph is used to monitor the progress of Iteration 1. It compares the ideal remaining work with the actual remaining work completed by the team.

## Iteration 1 Total Estimated Effort

The selected user stories for Iteration 1 have a total estimated effort of 7 days.

| User Story | Estimated Effort |
|---|---:|
| Create an account | 2 days |
| Log in securely | 2 days |
| Create reading goals | 3 days |
| **Total** | **7 days** |

## Burn Down Data

| Day | Ideal Remaining Work | Actual Remaining Work |
|---|---:|---:|
| Day 0 | 7 | 7 |
| Day 1 | 5.6 | 6 |
| Day 2 | 4.2 | 4 |
| Day 3 | 2.8 | 3 |
| Day 4 | 1.4 | 1 |
| Day 5 | 0 | 0 |

## Progress Explanation

The burn down graph shows that the team is progressing close to the planned schedule. At the beginning of Iteration 1, the total remaining work was 7 days. As development continued, the remaining work decreased steadily.

Although the actual remaining work was slightly higher than the ideal line on Day 1, the team caught up on Day 2 and continued to make stable progress. By Day 5, the actual remaining work reached 0, showing that the selected Iteration 1 tasks were completed according to the schedule.
